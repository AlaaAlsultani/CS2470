About Us

Pokemon Twitch Streaming Incorporated was founded in 2018 with the intent to merge the wonderful world of pokemon to a twitch stream. Pokemon fans have loved to see pokemon in so many different contexts throughout the years, so our focus is having pokemon entertain and bond with you, rather than the other way around. We work with pokemon of all types so don't by shy with your ghost pokemon or think your fire pokemon is too loud.
